**Superstore** **Dashboard**

A Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance and trends for a retail superstore. This project provides insights into sales, profits, product performance, and customer behavior.

**Project** **Overview**

**Objective:** To visualize and analyze superstore sales data for better decision-making.

**Dataset:** Superstore dataset containing sales, profits, product categories, regions, and customer details.

**Tools** **Used:** Power BI

**Insights:** The Superstore Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, profit, and quantity trends across categories, regions, and sub-categories. Clothing and Electronics lead in sales, while Furniture lags behind. The West and South regions show the highest sales, whereas the North region has room for growth. Monthly trends indicate peaks in April and August, with a notable dip in June affecting profits. Top-performing sub-categories include Shirts, Dresses, and Headphones, highlighting key revenue drivers. Overall, the dashboard helps identify high-performing areas, underperforming regions, and opportunities to optimize discounts and improve profitability.# Superstore-sales-Dashboard
